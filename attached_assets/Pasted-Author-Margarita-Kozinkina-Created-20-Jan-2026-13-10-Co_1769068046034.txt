Additionally, after reviewing your responses to the Pre-Integration Questionnaire, I would like to share our integration recommendations:

Retrieve bookings

In the questionnaire, you specified that you require the extraction of booking order details via API and expect the rate details to change during booking. 

Since you need to retrieve order details through the API, please be informed that our "Retrieve bookings" API method is meant to retrieve booking information {}after the booking process has been completed{}; {}it should not be used during the booking process ({}{}as the details of the rate won't change during booking, they can only change during prebooking{}{}){}. Due to internal synchronization processes, we cannot guarantee that a response from the "Retrieve bookings" method will be returned right after the order is confirmed. 

I want to note that the final booking status should be obtained through the "Check booking process" call or by receiving the booking status webhook. Only the "ok" status in the "Check booking process" response or "completed" status in the webhook should indicate successful order creation and booking confirmation. 

Please consider these points as you move forward with the integration.
